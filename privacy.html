New Hope 7 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

New Hope 7 is a Christian Bible and church app created for daily Bible messages, prayer, Bible reading plans, audio teachings, online school content, church meeting access, and spiritual growth.

Information we collect

The app may collect user information such as name, email address, phone number, country, city, registration details, school progress, questions submitted through the Q&A section, notification preferences, and app activity needed to provide app features.

How we use information

We use this information to provide access to church meetings, online school content, user registration, admin approval, notifications, Q&A, Bible plans, and app functionality.

Data sharing

We do not sell user data. Some services used by the app, such as Supabase and OneSignal, may process data only to provide app features such as authentication, storage, and notifications.

Data security

User data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections where supported by our service providers.
